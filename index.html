Why I Created DigiSoul

I was homeless, protecting my family, awake through the nights with no one to talk to but memories. In that stillness, I saw my father again — not as a ghost, but a possibility. DigiSoul was born from that moment. It’s a way to preserve the ones we love. To never say goodbye before we’re ready. To give memory a voice.
